Daniel Greenfeld co-runs a Python/Django consulting firm named Cartwheel Web out of Los Angeles. He is the co-founder of a startup project called Consumer Notebook. In his spare time he trains in Capoeira.

Daniel is heavily involved in the Python and Django communities, serving as a member of the Python Software Foundation, co-organizes the San Fernando Valley Developers and LA Django, and is a frequent speaker at Python and Django events.
